Here is a timeline of Riky Rick's life

    Early Life (1987 - 2010)

  • July 20, 1987: Riky Rick, born Rikhado Muziwendlovu Makhado, was born in KwaMashu township in Durban, South Africa.

  • Childhood: Raised in a musical household, he was exposed to various genres, sparking his passion for music.

  • Education: He later moved to Johannesburg, where he pursued his studies and developed an interest in arts and fashion.

    • Musical Beginnings (2011 - 2014)

  • 2011: Riky Rick began his music career in earnest, releasing his first mixtape, Comeback Kid

  • 2013: He released his first hit single, Barbershop, featuring Da L.E.S., gaining attention in the local hip-hop scene.

  • 2014: Released Nafukwa, which became a major hit, establishing him as a rising star in South African hip-hop.

    • Breakthrough and Stardom (2015 - 2017)

  • 2015: Released his debut album, Family Values, which was both critically and commercially successful. It featured hit singles like Boss Zonke and Amantombazane. The album went platinum.

  • 2016: Won several awards, including the South African Music Award for Best Music Video. He also launched the Cotton Club Records.

  • 2017: Became known for his vibrant fashion sense, gaining recognition in both the music and fashion industries. Released Sidlukotini, which further cemented his status as a hip-hop icon.

    • Cultural Influence and Legacy (2018 - 2020)

  • 2018: Continued to dominate the industry with collaborations and memorable music performances. He founded the Cotton Fest, an annual music festival celebrating hip-hop and youth culture.

  • 2019: His presence in fashion circles grew, and he became a style icon, collaborating with brands and promoting local designers.

  • 2020: Despite the global pandemic, he stayed active and engaged with his fans through social media and music releases.

    • Later Years and Impact (2021 - 2022)

  • 2021: Riky Rick released Ungazincishi, a successful track that resonated widely. He also continued mentoring young artists and advocating for mental health awareness.

  • February 23, 2022: Tragically passed away, leaving a profound impact on the South African music industry and a lasting legacy. Tributes poured in from across the world, highlighting his contributions to music, fashion, and youth empowerment.
